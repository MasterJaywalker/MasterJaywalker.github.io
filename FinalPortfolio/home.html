About Me

I am a born and raised Idahoan living in Nampa, Idaho. I graduated from Skyview in 2016 and went to CWI immediately after. I attended for two years focusing on general education and was able to take one semester of the Software Development course offered and found an interest in coding and web-design. Unfortunately several challenges presented themselves to me at that time and I had to take a hiatus from college.

After my short time at CWI I went on to become a plumber for an outfit in Nampa, a lube technician at Einstein's Oilery, and a bulk receiver at Darigold. I am a dog lover with two pups, Indigo and Auggie, and a loving fiance to my partner of four years, Ashlyn Fear. Through time and hard work I have earned everything I've ever wanted: my own pickup truck, a loving family, and purchased my first home in 2019.

I am now almost 25 and after working in the service industry for many years I know what I want in life. My goal is to pursue an education in Software Development and become a back-end programmer. I find enjoyment in the challenges object-oriented programming presents and I love problem-solving. I know that the path ahead of me will be difficult and will require hard work, but I have accomplished everything I've set out for myself thus-far and I'm ready for whatever comes my way.
